About Me

I'm a software developer with 8 years of experience creating custom web applications for small businesses. I don't work with templates or page builders—I build real software tailored to your business needs.

What do I build? Web tools that automate your business: appointment systems integrated with WhatsApp, inventory management platforms, customer databases, billing systems, and complete business management platforms. Everything web-based—access from any computer or phone with internet.

I specialize in understanding how small businesses operate and building tools that fit your workflow. Whether you need a simple appointment system or a complete business management platform, I can build it quickly and deliver exactly what you need.
